What is convolution?

For discrete‑time signals, convolution of x[n] and h[n] is defined as:

\( y[n] = \sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} x[k] h[n - k] \)

  • Describes output y[n] of an LTI system with impulse response h[n] and input x[n].
  • Graphical view: flip one signal in time, shift, multiply and sum over overlap.[web:228]
